Magazine Anandotsab   

Durga Puja is not just a festival; it's a vibrant tapestry of traditions, art, music, and devotion that brings us all together. Our magazine aims to capture the spirit of this celebration through the voices and perspectives of our dearest community members. We invite you to share your thoughts, experiences, stories, and creative expressions related to an open platform. We believe that each story shared, each artwork created, and each reflection penned will contribute to a magazine that reflects the collective heartbeat of our community during Durga Puja. Let's unite to celebrate the cultural fabric that binds us and let our voices resonate in this beautiful mosaic of Durga Puja events.
