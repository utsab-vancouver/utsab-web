It is our pleasure to invite all of you, your friends, guests and well-wishers to celebrate this year`s UTSAB’s Annual Cultural Program  on July 15, 2023 at Bonsor Recreation Complex  located at 6550 Bonsor Ave, Burnaby, BC V5H 2G8, from 5pm till 10pm.   

This event is a platform to showcase our kid`s talents, celebrate our unique culture and rich heritage. We all will celebrate the fun filled evening with music, cultural events and adda. We encourage you to bring your friends, guests and well-wishers to enjoy an evening that will include drama, music, entertainment, exciting raffle draw prizes, fun and food.

Bengali style snacks with tea and dinner will be served in the event. Subscriptions for the program (including food) are as follows:

 

  • Family : $50.00/family
  • Individual adult: $20.00/person
  • Student: $10.00/person

 

We look forward to seeing you at this event. Please RSVP
